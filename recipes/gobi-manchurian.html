Ingredients

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 spoon garam masala
  • 100 grams chickpeas
  • Lemon juice (optional)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Heat the oil

    Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.

  2. Add spices

    Add cumin seeds, ginger and garlic. Cook for 30 seconds.

  3. Add vegetables

    Add onion and tomatoes. Cook for 5-7 minutes until soft and slightly golden & thick.

  4. Add chickpeas

    Add chickpeas, coat them in gravy. Cook for 15-20 minutes.

  5. Garnish & serve

    Top with fresh cilantro.
